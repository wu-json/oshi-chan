MASHLE: MAGIC AND MUSCLES

MASHLE; MASHLE: MAGIC AND MUSCLES
PG 13 HD
In a world where magic reigns supreme, Mash Burnedead suffers from the disadvantage of being young and without power. Perceived as a danger to the purity of the gene pool, he's forced to hide in the forest and dedicate himself to daily physical training. His goal: to develop the strength necessary to rival the power of magic.
But when his true identity is revealed and his very existence threatened, Mash embarks on a mission to become a Divine Visionary - a role of such importance that society will have no choice but to accept him. To achieve this feat, Mash must first enroll in the prestigious Easton Magic Academy, competing against children from the most influential families in the realm.
Lacking the magical abilities required to succeed at the academy, Mash is already at a disadvantage compared to his peers. Nevertheless, he perseveres and commits to using his physical strength and fighting skills to pass every trial and overcome the challenges presented by his classmates' magical abilities. For Mash, the title of Divine Visionary is worth any obstacle that may come his way.

    2nd Offense Within 30 Days of 1st Offense: Permanent Ban

    Major Spoiler:-

    1st Offense: Permanent Ban
